\\ Samee Gold & Diamonds

Samee Gold & Diamonds is a South Indian jewelry chain that sells gold and diamond jewelry. It was founded in 1985 and is based in Chennai, Tamil Nadu. Features
Showrooms Samee Gold & Diamonds has many showrooms across South India, including one of the largest jewelry showrooms in the world in Hyderabad. Products
Samee Gold & Diamonds sells a wide range of jewelry, including gold jewelry, diamond jewelry, and South Indian jewelry collections. Quality
Samee Gold & Diamonds BIS 916 Hallmarked gold jewelry and diamond jewelry with authenticity certificates from internationally accredited Gemmology bodies. Pricing
Samee Gold & Diamonds claims to have the lowest VAT charges in the market and aims to eliminate the need for bargaining. Services
Click and compare: Customers can take a picture of their dream jewel on their mobile to compare prices across different showrooms. Video shopping: Customers can browse, select, and shop for their favorite jewelry from Ours with a video call feature.
